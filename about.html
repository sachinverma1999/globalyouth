ABOUT US

AIMS AND OBJECTIVES

  1. Engage and energise young minds in generating informed opinions and ideas on foreign policy imperatives and global challenges
  2. Connect the youth of India with foreign policy circles, global thinkers and global leaders
  3. Demystify the intricacies and subtleties of foreign policy formulation and management
  4. Challenge and encourage young minds to think of out-of-the-box ideas on global issues
  5. Enthuse and Inform young Indians of the skill sets and career paths needed to be successful contributors in international organisations and think tanks, both governmental and non-governmental
  6. Facilitate opportunities for young students to gain practical work experience with global organisations
  7. Expose young minds to ideas and perspectives from around the globe
  8. Establish a forum for young Indians to better understand how the world sees India and how other nations are looking to engage with India
  9. Generate an atmosphere where the youth understand and help develop a spirit of international partnership on gbbal issues
  10. Formulate systems to encourage the pursuit of education and cross-ideological knowledge enhancement in Foreign Policy
