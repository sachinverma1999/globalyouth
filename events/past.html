OUR PAST EVENTS
1. Global Youth conducted the 3rd edition of the Global Youth Annual Summit on 3 April 2019. The theme of the Summit was "Changing Global Paradigms and the Emergence of Youth." The event witnessed the participation of over 100 students from across Delhi and was held at the India International Centre.


2. Global Youth conducted an international conference with a delegation of 14 students from Moscow, Russia. The sessions were conducted at the India International Centre, in collaboration with the India-Russia Youth Forum, Global Youth.


3. Global Youth recently conducted a "Chat with a Diplomat" at Hansraj College, Delhi University, in collaboration with the Embassy of France in New Delhi and the India-France Youth Forum, Global Youth. The guest of the hour was Mr. Simon Horrenberger, Counsellor at the French Embassy. The event featured a talk by Mr. Horrenberger, followed by an interactive session with the participants.


4. Global Youth conducted a "Chat with a Diplomat" at the Residence of the Hon'ble Ambassador of the Netherlands to India, Nepal and Bhutan, Mr. Marten Van Den Berg. The event was also graced by Michiel Bierkens, Head of Economic Cluster at the Netherlands Embassy and Ms. Karanpreet Kaur, Senior Policy Officer of Political Affairs, Human Rights and Education at the Embassy.


5. Global Youth conducted a "Team Day" for all members of Global Youth on 3 March 2019 at Nehru Park. The event was conducted to bring together various teams working under the organisation and celebrate with games, food and fun.
