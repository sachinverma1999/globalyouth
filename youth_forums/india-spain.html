India - Spain Youth Forum

India Spain Youth Forum is a part of the large family of Global Youth and aims to provide a platform for Indian and Spanish counterparts to discuss and debate on international relations and foreign policy. The forum is a proud group of students from diverse backgrounds yet interwined with a common passion for diplomacy and foreign relations. We together work and strive towards a common goal of holistic analytical development. With our wide diapora of activities, members are engaged in a multidimensional exchange of culture and dialogue.
