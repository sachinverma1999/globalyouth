About the Chapter
Established in 2017, the KNC chapter of Global Youth aims at helping KNCites gain exposure of meeting the best minds and foreign think-tanks, and to further have a global perspective and better understanding of the contemporary issues that concern international relations and world affairs.

Our Goals
  • To benefit as many students as possible in career building.
  • To provide exposure opportunities as possible.
  • To help them think big, think global.
