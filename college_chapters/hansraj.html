About the Chapter
Established in 2018, the Hansraj Chapter of Global Youth was established with a view to attain insights into foreign and public policy. A plethora of events were conducted in accordance with the major agenda of the organisation, moulded by a team of dedicated individuals.

Our Goals
  • To bridge the gap of positives and normatives by spreading information and knowledge.
  • To set an example for future position holders to work collectively and diligently.
